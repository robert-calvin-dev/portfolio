Why Your Website Might Look Great… But Still Be Invisible

You’ve spent thousands on web design. Your branding is clean, your copy’s tight, and yet… no traffic. No conversions. Crickets.

Here’s the truth most agencies won’t tell you: if your SEO foundation is cracked, no amount of pretty pixels will save you.

An SEO audit is not just about keywords. It’s about performance, structure, code, and trust. And I don’t run audits using generic SaaS checkers—I rip your site apart like a mechanic pulls apart an engine.

The 5 Pillars of a Real SEO Audit

🧱 Technical Foundation

  • Crawl errors
  • Broken links
  • Redirect chains
  • Canonical conflicts
  • Robots.txt and sitemap.xml issues
  • Mobile responsiveness
  • HTTPS/SSL validation

Tools: Screaming Frog, Sitebulb, Python scripts

📄 On-Page SEO

  • Heading misuse
  • Duplicate/missing meta tags
  • Keyword-content alignment
  • Content hierarchy
  • Thin or duplicate content

Tools: Ahrefs, SurferSEO, manual analysis

⚙️ Site Performance

  • Core Web Vitals
  • Render-blocking resources
  • Image optimization
  • Hosting & CDN checks

Tools: Lighthouse, GTmetrix, WebPageTest

🔗 Off-Page & Trust Signals

  • Backlink analysis
  • Brand mentions
  • Reputation metrics
  • Domain history and penalties

Tools: Ahrefs, Moz, manual checks

🧐 UX & Intent Alignment

  • Bounce rate
  • Click paths
  • CTA clarity
  • Device behavior differences
